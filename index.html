About Me

Bios

My name is Reina and I am a user interface designer. I am currently studying Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University.

I am passionate about creating user friendly platforms in different areas of web, especially for game industries. My long-term goal is to work as a UI designer for game platforms where users can upload, purchase, and download games.

As a designer and a gamer, I thrive to create web spaces that users can navigate as if they are playing a game.
